Web Developer Internship Opportunities at Cognifyz Technologies

At Cognifyz Technologies, our Web Development Internship Program is designed to provide hands-on training and industry-level experience to budding developers. The program focuses on practical learning through real-world projects and expert guidance.


  • Comprehensive training in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and Node.js
  • Hands-on experience through live projects and assignments
  • Exposure to responsive web design and user experience principles
